We are tapt.

A social media web ap that combines the power of combining owners of bars serving craft beers to the people who love to drink them. The app allows for an owner of an establishment the power to change their inventory and let the masses know what is currently on tap.

The general user of the app is allowed to rank beers tehy have come across on their travels, rank the establishment itself, and tag beers they are interest in and be notified when the beer is back on tap. Users can also find terrific resources on the site to educate them about all of the terrific beers types that actually exist in the world.

Did we also mention that you can create a customized pub crawl for you and your friends to share with the world? Yeah, we did it. You're welcome.
